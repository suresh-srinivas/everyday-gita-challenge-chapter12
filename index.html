Welcome to the EveryDay G.I.T.A Challenge!

At the conclusion of every chapter, we will have an EveryDay G.I.T.A Challenge to provide opportunities for youth and others to share their audio or video. If you are looking to participate in the challenge, record and share with us:

  1. The whole chapter or a verse or two that you connect to
  2. Your picture
  3. Your name
  4. If you are a student, your class/age, and if you are an adult, your profession
  5. The audio recording in mp3 or m4a format. You can also share a video in mp4 if you like

Our sincere thanks to everyone that participated and all the teachers (Swati garu, Vinayagopal of MGM) for encouraging their students to participate, and the parents of the youth.

Special thanks to Anika a college student for helping put the website together.

Please listen and support the participants on their journey.

With the blessings of Sri Krishna, Gita Maata, and our revered Gurus, Sri K.V. Shivaswamy and Dr. Venkata Raju, we are pleased to begin the second cycle of EveryDay G.I.T.A. (Everyday Gita In Text and Audio) Yagna.

This journey started on May 17th, 2022, following nearly five months of daily sessions with Dr. Raju. We successfully completed the first cycle on April 26th, 2024. Each day, you will receive a verse from the Bhagavad Gita along with an audio recitation by one of our dedicated volunteers.

At the end of each chapter, we will host a community challenge. This web page showcases the results of those challenges.
